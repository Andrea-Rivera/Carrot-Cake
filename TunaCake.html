Tuna Cake

Ingredients

  • 250 grams of shredded cheese
  • 6 slices of bread
  • 2 cans of tuna
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 red capsicum (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon of white self raising flour and butter to spread in the cake pan /li>

Recipe

  1. In a bowl, grind the slices of bread
  2. Pour the milk onto the bowl and mix the ingredients.
  3. Once the bread is wet with the milk, add the eggs.
  4. Then, add the cheese, the capsicum and the tuna.Mixed up all the ingredients
  5. Use a cake pan and spread a bit of self raising flour and butter on it.
  6. Add the mix onto the cake pan and put it into the oven. Leave it for about 20 minutes.

    7. COOKING TIPS!!! Do not leave the oven unnatended. When you open and close the door, do it slowly.
    To know if the cake is ready, take a knife and sink it into the cake. When the knife emerge clean, the cake is ready. Enjoy!!
