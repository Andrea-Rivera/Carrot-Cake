Carrot Cake

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 3 carrots
  • 1/2 cup of oil
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 2 cups of white self raising flour

Recipe

  1. Peel 3 carrots and cut them into slices.
  2. Put into the blender the eggs, carrots, oil, sugar and blend everything until dissolved.
  3. Preheat the oven at 180 degrees.
  4. In a bowl, pour all the mix from the blender and mix it with two cups of flour.
  5. Use a cake pan and spread a bit of flour on it.
  6. Add the mix onto the cake pan and put it into the oven. Leave it for about 20 minutes.

    7. Instructions using the oven: Leave only one tray in the oven and position it as close to the bottom of the oven as possible. Use the conventional heating symbol

    COOKING TIPS!!! Do not leave the oven unnatended. When you open and close the door, do it slowly.
    To know if the cake is ready, take a knife and sink it into the cake. When the knife emerge clean, the cake is ready. Enjoy!!
